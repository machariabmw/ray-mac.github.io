Another Section

During my primary school, there were no counties nor devolved governance. The entire state was headed by the national government. It was otherwise associated with less cases of corruption. The various locations of governance were in a specific hierachy as shown below.

  1. Village - headed by elders
  2. Sub-location - headed by sub-chie
  3. Location - headed by chief
  4. Division - headed by District Officer (D.O)
  5. District - headed by District Commissioner (D.C)
  6. Province - headed by Provincial Commissioner
  7. Country/State/Nation - Headed by President
